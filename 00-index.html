I am a recent PhD graduate from Autonomy Lab at School of Computing Science, Simon Fraser University (SFU). I study human-robot interaction (HRI) and my research is focused on designing multimodal interaction systems. I have developed methods for humans to get the robot's attention in simple and intuitive ways. My research interests are sensor fusion, design of the robot behavior, human-robot interaction, and their applications to domains where robots can work with and around people in their everyday life.
