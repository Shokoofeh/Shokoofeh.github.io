I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Dr. Richard Vaughan in the Autonomy Lab at the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University (SFU). Currently, I study human-robot interaction (HRI) and my research is focused on designing multimodal interaction systems. I have developed methods for humans to get the robot's attention in simple and intuitive ways.
